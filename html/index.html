Want to help fight climate change?
We want to see a future where Nemo can just keep swimming in coral reefs and penguins can march on ice caps for decades to come. At this point, those visions are looking more like an actual Disney fairytale than our reality.

According to the IPCC (the United Nations body for assessing the science related to climate change) they found that the world needs to take urgent, transformative action to avert the worst impacts of climate change beyond 1.5°C (or 2.7° Fahrenheit for y'all non-metric converting American) degrees warming.

This is a big deal! And it can't happen overnight or with one wave of a magic wand (but like could we phone a friend and get Harry Potter to work on this too?). We need dramatic policy changes, large companies committing to to going carbon neutral (or negative! like Microsoft plans to), citizen voices demanding change, and new technology to remove carbon from the air.

While that might feel like an overwhelming pie to have a slice of, you can start small. Here's a few of our favorite resources to help reduce your carbon footprint as an individual AND start speaking up as a voice for change in your community.

Here's one tiny action that could help speak up and get Uber to commit to going carbon neutral (like Lyft already has. Yay!). This one tweet will not make the climate change pie look less gloomy, but it could be a delicious red chili flake. Start small, but don't stop small people.
