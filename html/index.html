Want to help fight climate change?
We want to see a future where Nemo can just keep swimming in coral reefs and penguins can march on ice caps for decades to come. However, these visions are starting to sound truly like a Disney fairytale than our predicted reality.

We are currently emitting about 45 billion tons of CO2 a year. Your personal Uber history likely contributes ~.000000000025% to total carbon emissions. We want to be clear: offsetting your Ubers will not solve this problem.

We need dramatic policy changes, large companies committing to going carbon neutral, citizen voices demanding change, and new technology to remove carbon from the air. Here is a link to some of our favorite resources to learn more and take action.

And in the meantime- let's create a climate tweetstorm at Uber to commit to going carbon neutral (like Lyft already has).
